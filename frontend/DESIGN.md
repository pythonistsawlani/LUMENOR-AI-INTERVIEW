# HireFlow AI Design System

## Global Styles
- **Theme:** Dark mode by default. Minimalist, futuristic, premium SaaS feel.
- **Inspiration:** Linear, Vercel, Stripe, Framer.
- **Typography:** Inter or similar clean sans-serif. Modern typographic hierarchy with tight tracking on headings.
- **Backgrounds:** Deep slate/black backgrounds with subtle animated gradients (indigo, purple, pink) or floating blur effects in the background.
- **Surfaces/Cards:** Glassmorphism. `bg-white/5` or `bg-slate-900/50`, with `backdrop-blur-xl`, `border-white/10`, and soft shadows.
- **Interactive Elements:** Smooth hover states (`transition-all duration-300`, `hover:scale-[1.02]`), micro-interactions on buttons.

## Components
- **Buttons:** 
  - Primary: Gradient backgrounds or solid sleek colors (indigo) with glowing shadows.
  - Secondary: Glass outline buttons with subtle hover backgrounds.
- **Cards (Feature/Candidate):** 
  - Soft borders, glowing hover effects, minimal padding.
- **Navbar:** Floating, glassmorphic top navigation bar.

## Colors
- **Primary:** Deep Indigo (`#4F46E5`) to Purple gradients.
- **Background:** Slate 950 (`#020617`) or Black.
- **Surface:** Slate 900 (`#0F172A`) with opacity.
- **Text:** Slate 50 (`#F8FAFC`) for headings, Slate 400 (`#94A3B8`) for body.

## Layout
- **Containers:** Max width 7xl, centered, proper whitespace and padding (`py-24` for sections).
